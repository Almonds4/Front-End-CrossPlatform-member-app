📬 Contact Us

We’d love to hear from you! Whether you have questions, feedback, or want to get involved with IIAEMS, we’re here to help.

📧 General Inquiries

For general questions, suggestions, or partnership opportunities, please reach out to us at: help@iiaems.org

We aim to respond within 2–3 business days. Thank you for your patience — your message matters to us!


🚨 Need Immediate Assistance?

If you’re a registered member or student and need urgent support, please contact: support@iiaems.org

We prioritize student and member inquiries and will do our best to respond as soon as possible.

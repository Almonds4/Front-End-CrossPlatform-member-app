What is IIAEMS?

Empowering Learners Worldwide

Founded in 2019, IIAEMS has grown from a small startup to a global education platform serving over 1 million students. What started as a simple idea has evolved into a comprehensive learning ecosystem that connects students with a team of expert educators, industry professionals, and researchers.

At IIAEMS, we create the corporate shared by a lifelong journey, not just an academic credential. Our platform is designed to bridge the gap between conventional education and real-world skills practices.

As learners, we review the corporate shared by a lifelong journey, not just an academic credential. That's why our courses are thoughtfully designed to help students learn at their own pace.
